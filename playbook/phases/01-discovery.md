# Phase 1: Discovery

**Objective:** Establish scope, build stakeholder relationships, and collect the information needed to plan the engagement correctly.

---

## Entry Criteria

- Signed SOW and MSA on file
- Initial payment received (if required per payment terms)
- Primary client contact identified

---

## Activities

### 1.1 Send Discovery Questionnaire
- Email the discovery questionnaire to the client's primary contact within 24 hours of contract execution
- Request completion within 5 business days
- Follow up at day 3 if not received

### 1.2 Schedule Discovery Call
- Book a 60–90 minute video call with the primary stakeholder and any technical contacts
- Agenda: business context, environment overview, key risks and concerns, preliminary scope walkthrough

### 1.3 Conduct Discovery Call
- Walk through questionnaire responses
- Clarify ambiguities on scope
- Identify CUI systems and data flows (CMMC engagements)
- Confirm stakeholder availability for interviews and data collection
- Document all open items

### 1.4 Collect Initial Documentation
Request at minimum:
- Asset inventory (systems, servers, endpoints, cloud services)
- Network diagram
- Org chart (for compliance engagements)
- Existing security policies (if any)
- Previous assessment reports (if any)

### 1.5 Finalize Scope Boundary
- Document all in-scope and out-of-scope assets, systems, and locations
- Get written confirmation from client (email is sufficient)

---

## Outputs

- Completed discovery questionnaire
- Discovery call notes
- Documented scope boundary (written client confirmation)
- Initial asset inventory
- Stakeholder contact list with roles

---

## Deliverables to Client

None — this is an internal phase. The kickoff agenda delivered at the start of Phase 2 is the first client-facing deliverable.

---

## Quality Gate

- [ ] Scope is agreed upon in writing before Phase 2 begins
- [ ] No ambiguity about what systems or locations are in or out of scope
- [ ] At least one technical contact and one business contact identified
- [ ] Initial documentation received or a collection plan is in place

## Common Issues

**Client doesn't return questionnaire:** Call instead of waiting. Walk through it verbally and document responses yourself.

**Scope keeps expanding:** Flag immediately. If scope expands beyond SOW, issue a change order before proceeding.

**No network diagram exists:** Document this as a gap finding and proceed with what the client can describe verbally. Request a hand-drawn diagram if necessary.

## Risk Scoring Key

| Likelihood | Definition |
|---|---|
| High | Likely to occur within the next 12 months based on current controls and threat landscape |
| Medium | Possible within the next 12–36 months |
| Low | Unlikely but possible |

| Impact | Definition |
|---|---|
| High | Significant financial loss, operational disruption, or regulatory penalty |
| Medium | Moderate disruption, limited financial exposure, recoverable |
| Low | Minimal disruption, easily contained |

| Risk Level | Likelihood x Impact |
|---|---|
| Critical | High x High |
| High | High x Medium or Medium x High |
| Medium | Medium x Medium, High x Low, or Low x High |
| Low | Low x Low or Low x Medium |

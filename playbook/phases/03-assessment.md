# Phase 3: Assessment

**Objective:** Execute the technical or documentary assessment per the service runbook.

---

## Entry Criteria

- Signed ROE on file (technical assessments)
- All required access provisioned
- Project schedule confirmed
- Kickoff meeting completed

---

## Activities

Activities vary significantly by service. Refer to the relevant service runbook for the specific assessment checklist. The activities below apply to all engagements.

### 3.1 Weekly Status Reporting
- Send a status report every week the engagement is active
- Include: activities completed, activities planned, open items, any issues or risks

### 3.2 Real-Time Critical Finding Notification
- If a Critical finding is identified during assessment, notify the client's primary contact immediately — do not wait for the weekly report
- Document the notification (email preferred for audit trail)

### 3.3 Evidence Cataloging
- Every piece of evidence collected must be logged in the evidence tracker
- Naming convention: `{ControlID or FindingID}_{Description}_{Date}.{ext}`
- Store in the engagement folder under `03-assessment/evidence/`

### 3.4 Interview Execution
- Follow the interview guide for the applicable service
- Take contemporaneous notes
- Send a brief follow-up summary to the interviewee for confirmation within 24 hours

### 3.5 Running Findings Log
- Maintain a running log of findings as they are identified
- Do not wait until the end of assessment to document — capture in real time
- Include: date identified, system/control affected, description, preliminary severity, evidence reference

---

## Outputs

- Evidence library (organized per naming convention)
- Running findings log
- Interview notes
- Weekly status reports sent

---

## Deliverables to Client

- Weekly status report

---

## Quality Gate

- [ ] All planned assessment activities completed per the service runbook
- [ ] Evidence catalog is complete with no orphaned evidence
- [ ] All stakeholder interviews completed
- [ ] No outstanding access or data collection issues
- [ ] Running findings log has no entries marked "TBD" or "needs validation"

## Common Issues

**Client misses scheduled interviews:** Reschedule within 48 hours. If repeated, escalate to primary contact. Document missed interviews in the status report.

**Scanning causes unintended disruption:** Stop scanning immediately. Notify client. Review ROE to confirm authorization. Document the incident. Resume only after client confirmation.

**Evidence doesn't match documentation:** Flag the discrepancy in the findings log. Do not assume either version is correct. Interview the relevant stakeholder.

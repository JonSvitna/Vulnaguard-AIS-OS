Company Overview

Vulnaguard LLC is a Maryland-based cybersecurity and compliance company focused on helping organizations reduce cyber risk, strengthen security programs, and prepare for government and regulated-industry compliance requirements. Our proprietary Sentinel platform combines compliance automation with executive risk visibility.

Mission

To help organizations strengthen their cybersecurity posture through practical security consulting and intelligent compliance automation.

Differentiators

  • Purpose-built for government contractors and their supply chain.
  • Proprietary Sentinel compliance intelligence platform.
  • API-first architecture — integrates with existing stacks.
  • Practical, risk-first approach — no compliance theater.
  • Combines consulting expertise with automation at micro-purchase pricing.

Core Competencies

  • CMMC Readiness AssessmentsLevel 1 & Level 2 gap analysis, scoping, evidence prep.
  • NIST SP 800-171 Gap AssessmentsControl-by-control review, scoring, POA&M drafting.
  • Vulnerability AssessmentsExternal & internal scans, prioritized remediation plans.
  • Compliance Automation & Continuous MonitoringEvidence pipelines, control telemetry, drift detection.
  • Risk Assessments & Risk RegistersQuantified risk scoring, register build, treatment plans.
  • Executive Cybersecurity ReportingBoard-ready dashboards, revenue-at-risk summaries.
  • Security Policy DevelopmentSSPs, policies, SOPs aligned to NIST & CMMC.
  • Governance, Risk & Compliance (GRC) SupportProgram build-out, vendor risk, audit liaison.

## 1. Overview

This Statement of Work ("SOW") is entered into by and between Vulnaguard ("Vulnaguard") and AfterSwing ("Client") and is governed by the Master Services Agreement ("MSA") between the parties, to be executed concurrently.

This SOW describes the scope, deliverables, timeline, and fees for the NIST SP 800-171 Gap Assessment described herein.

---

## 2. Services

Vulnaguard will perform a NIST SP 800-171 Gap Assessment for AfterSwing. The engagement will be conducted in accordance with the Vulnaguard Delivery Methodology and NIST SP 800-171 Rev 2.

### 2.1 In Scope

All IT systems within AfterSwing's CUI boundary, including associated policies, procedures, and personnel.

Specifically, the following are in scope:
- All systems that create, receive, store, process, or transmit CUI
- Security policies, procedures, and documentation governing in-scope systems
- IT and security personnel responsible for in-scope systems

### 2.2 Out of Scope

The following are explicitly out of scope:
- Systems outside AfterSwing's defined CUI boundary
- Third-party SaaS applications not directly handling CUI
- Any systems, locations, or activities not listed as in scope above

### 2.3 Client Responsibilities

Client agrees to:
- Provide accurate and timely information during the discovery phase
- Provide access to systems, documentation, and personnel as required
- Review and respond to draft deliverables within 5 business days
- Designate a primary contact and technical contact for the engagement

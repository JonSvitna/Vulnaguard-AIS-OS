## 2. Roles & Workshare

The Prime will (a) serve as prime contractor and primary point of contact
with the customer, (b) lead proposal development, and (c) hold the prime
contract if awarded.

### 2.2 Team Member

Vulnaguard will serve as a **proposed subcontractor** for the
work-share described below, contingent on (a) award to the Prime,
(b) execution of a definitive Subcontract Agreement on terms substantially
consistent with this Agreement, and (c) Customer consent (if required by
the solicitation).

## 3. Proposal Responsibilities

### 3.1 Vulnaguard's contributions

Vulnaguard will provide (at no charge to the Prime):
- Technical content covering its workshare;
- Past-performance references and qualifications;
- Pricing inputs based on Vulnaguard Government Pricing Matrix v1.0;
- Required corporate certifications (SAM.gov registration, CAGE 21QQ7,
  UEI CJ7ZXTUSN3W8, NAICS confirmations).

### 3.2 Prime's contributions

The Prime will (a) lead overall proposal architecture, win-theme
development, and review, (b) integrate Vulnaguard inputs without material
change without consultation, (c) provide a copy of the proposal in final
form to Vulnaguard, and (d) handle solicitation Q&A and final submission.

### 3.3 Proposal Costs

Each Party bears its own proposal preparation costs. Neither Party is
entitled to reimbursement absent award and a definitive Subcontract
Agreement.

---

## 4. Exclusivity

During the term of this Agreement, **neither Party** will (a) submit a
proposal on the Opportunity with or as a competitor of the other, (b) team
with a competitor of the other for the Opportunity, or (c) act in a manner
that materially harms the other's competitive position on the Opportunity.

Vulnaguard may continue to (i) pursue other opportunities with other
primes, (ii) sell its standard products and services (including the
Sentinel platform) to other customers, and (iii) team for opportunities
**not** covered by this Agreement.

## 5. Confidentiality

Information exchanged between the Parties to pursue the Opportunity is
**Confidential Information** subject to the mutual NDA between the Parties
dated `[YYYY-MM-DD]` (or, if none, the confidentiality terms of any MSA
between the Parties). If no separate confidentiality agreement is in
place, the confidentiality terms of the Vulnaguard standard mutual NDA
apply by reference and are deemed incorporated into this Agreement.

---

## 6. Subcontract Negotiation

If the Prime is awarded the prime contract, the Parties will negotiate in
good faith a definitive Subcontract Agreement within **thirty (30) days**
of award. The Subcontract will reflect (a) the workshare in §2.3 as
adjusted by award scope, (b) Vulnaguard's standard rates per the Pricing
Matrix referenced above, (c) applicable FAR / DFARS flow-down clauses,
and (d) the prime contract's period of performance and option years.

If the Parties cannot reach a Subcontract Agreement within the negotiation
window despite good-faith efforts, the Prime may engage another
subcontractor for the work, subject to Customer consent and applicable
small-business subcontracting commitments.

---

## 7. Term & Termination

This Agreement begins on the Effective Date and ends on the earliest of:
(a) execution of a definitive Subcontract Agreement; (b) the customer's
written notice of award to a competing offeror; (c) cancellation of the
Opportunity by the customer; (d) `[twelve (12) months]` after the
Effective Date; or (e) written agreement of the Parties.

Either Party may terminate for material breach uncured within fifteen
(15) days of written notice. Sections 4 (Exclusivity), 5
(Confidentiality), 8 (Publicity), and 10 (General) survive termination.

---

## 8. Publicity

Neither Party will issue press releases or make public statements
referencing the other Party in connection with the Opportunity without
the other Party's prior written consent, except as required by law or
by customer-required reporting.

---

## 9. Compliance

Each Party will comply with applicable laws and the terms of the
solicitation, including the Procurement Integrity Act and applicable
Organizational Conflict of Interest (OCI) requirements. Each Party
represents that it is not currently debarred, suspended, or proposed
for debarment from federal contracting, and will promptly notify the
other if its status changes.

---

## 10. General

a. **Independent Parties.** Each Party is an independent contractor; this
   Agreement does not create a partnership, joint venture, agency, or
   fiduciary relationship.
b. **No Subcontract Until Signed.** No subcontract obligation arises until
   a definitive Subcontract Agreement is executed.
c. **Assignment.** Neither Party may assign without the other's prior
   written consent.
d. **Notices.** Effective when sent to the addresses above by email with
   receipt confirmation or by overnight courier.
e. **Entire Agreement; Amendments.** This Agreement is the entire
   agreement on its subject and supersedes prior discussions. Amendments
   require a writing signed by both Parties.
f. **Counterparts; e-Signature.** Electronic signature accepted.
g. **Governing Law & Forum.** Governed by the laws of **Maryland** without
   regard to conflict-of-laws rules. Exclusive forum: state and federal
   courts in `[county, MD]`, except either Party may seek injunctive
   relief elsewhere.

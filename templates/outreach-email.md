# Cold Outreach Email Template

Structure only — voice/tone comes from the `seanbuilds-voice` skill (auto-triggers on drafting tasks). Per `CLAUDE.md`, show Sean a draft before sending anything externally.

```
Subject: {short, specific, no buzzwords}

Hi {first name},

{one line — why you're reaching out to THEM specifically, not a template}

{one line — the value prop, framed as curiosity/peer-to-peer, not a pitch}

{CTA — one ask, low friction (e.g. "worth a 15-min call?")}

{sign-off}
Sean
```

After sending, add a row to `references/outreach-log.md` the same day.

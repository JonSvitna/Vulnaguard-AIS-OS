**Hook:** Every render since the storyboard deploy was silently failing — moving duration probing from webpack's browser bundle to plain Node ffprobe fixed 100% of them.
**Talking points:**
1. The root cause was a transitive dependency chain: @remotion/renderer → extract-zip → yauzl → buffer-crc32 — all needing Node builtins that webpack's browser-target bundle can't resolve, killing every composition, not just the one that used the import.

3. The fix was architectural: move the ffprobe call entirely out of the bundled code into the render-worker (plain Node, no webpack involved) and pass sourceDurationInSeconds in as a prop, keeping calculateMetadata pure arithmetic with zero dangerous imports.
